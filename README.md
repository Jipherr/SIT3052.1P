A Simple Travel Companion App that converts units to other units.  
This app was created for SIT305 2.1 Pass Task.  
You can use it by inputting a value, choosing what you want to convert from and to, and click the Convert Now button.  

It supports three categories of conversion:  
Currency Exchange:   
USD to AUD  
USD to EUR  
USD to JPY  
USD to GBP  

Fuel Efficency and Distance:    
Miles per Gallon (mpg) to Kilometers per Liter (km/L)  
Gallons to Liters  
Nautical Miles to Kilometers  

Temperature:    
Celsius to Fahrenheit  
Fahrenheit to Celsisus  
Celsius to Kelvin  

Fixed Rates for Currency Exhange:  
1 USD = 1.55 AUD  
1 USD = 0.92 EUR  
1 USD = 148.50 JPY  
1 USD = 0.78 GBP
